Flickmoji Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

Flickmoji ("the app") is a guess-the-movie-from-emojis game published by Hedyverse, LLC ("we," "us"). We built Flickmoji to be a lightweight, privacy-respecting game. This policy explains what data is and isn't collected when you play.

The short version

  • Flickmoji has no accounts and no login. We do not ask for your name, email, or any personal details.
  • Your game progress is stored on your device (and in your own iCloud, if you have it enabled) — not on our servers.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites, and the app does not request access to the advertising identifier (IDFA).
  • The only data collected is what our advertising partner needs to show ads, described below.

Advertising

Flickmoji shows optional rewarded video ads (you choose to watch one to earn in-game tickets) served by Google AdMob. To serve these ads, the Google Mobile Ads SDK may collect a device identifier, ad-interaction and advertising data, and technical diagnostics such as crash and performance data. Because the app does not request tracking permission, these ads are served on a non-personalized basis and this data is not linked to your identity and not used to track you. Google's handling of this data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy.

Purchases

Flickmoji offers optional in-app purchases (coin packs). All payments are processed by Apple through the App Store. We never see or store your payment card, and we do not receive your name or billing details.

Apple Game Center & iCloud

If you choose to use Game Center (for leaderboards and achievements) or iCloud (to sync your progress across devices), those features are provided by Apple and governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. Using them is optional and controlled by your device settings.

Children

Flickmoji is rated for ages 4+ and is suitable for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because the app has no accounts and collects no personal data, it does not build user profiles of any player, regardless of age.

Your choices

You can reset all locally stored progress by deleting the app. You can limit ad personalization system-wide in your device's Settings → Privacy & Security. Because we hold no personal data about you, there is no account for us to delete.

Changes

If we update this policy, we'll revise the date at the top of this page. Material changes will be reflected in the app's App Store listing.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at support@flickmoji.app.
